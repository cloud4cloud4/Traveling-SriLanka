North Western Province

History

The North Western Province (Wayamba) offers a rural landscape with coconut plantations and domestic wildlife in three parks/sanctuaries (part of Wilpattu National Park, Kahalla-Pallekele Sanctuary and Mundel Bird Sanctuary).There are stretches of sandy beaches with peaceful lagoons and the remains of four medieval capitals of Sri Lanka: Panduwasnuwara, Dambadeniya, Yapahuwa, and Kurunegala.The largest city and capital is Kurunegala, a major commercial and transportation hub in the region.

Attractions

Some of the top attractions places include:

  • Kudawa Beach
  • Athugala Rock
  • Kalpitiya Fort
  • Baththalanguduwa
